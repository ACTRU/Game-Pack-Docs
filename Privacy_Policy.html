Privacy Policy for MIND GamePack

Last updated: October 21, 2025 Version: 3.0

The MIND GamePack application is used exclusively for research purposes and is distributed privately to study participants on provisioned devices managed by Massachusetts General Hospital (MGH). By having access to this application, you have given informed consent to the research team behind the MIND GamePack to utilize the application on said devices. This research is governed by the MGH Institutional Review Board (IRB) and is subject to adherence to Good Clinical Practices (GCP) in clinical research. You may withdraw consent at any time. Your participation in this research is voluntary.

What Data We Collect

  • De-identified gameplay data and survey responses related to cognitive performance.
  • No personally identifiable information (PII) or personal health information (PHI) is collected or stored by the application.

How Data Is Stored

  • Data is transmitted securely to servers managed by our research facility managed by Massachusetts General Hospital, including a local secure database and a Google Cloud instance.
  • Data may be temporarily cached on the device if a Wi-Fi connection is unavailable, and will be transmitted once a connection is restored.

How Data Is Used and Shared

  • De-identified data is used for research purposes, to which you have consented.
  • By participating, you consent to your de-identified data being shared with third parties and other researchers for scientific purposes.
  • At this time, no data is used for commercial purposes.
  • If you have any other further questions on how your data is utilized, stored, or shared, please refer to a signed copy of your consent form or reach out to the study team below for any specific questions.

Biometric Authentication

  • The application uses Face ID or Touch ID (if available on your device) to verify your identity and secure access to the application.
  • Biometric data is processed locally on your device and is never transmitted to our servers or stored by the application.

Withdrawal and Data Deletion

  • You may stop using the application and withdraw your consent at any time by notifying the study team.
  • Upon withdrawal, no further data will be collected. You may request deletion of your data, though all data is de-identified and cannot be linked to you personally.

Children's Privacy

  • At this time, children under the age of 18 will not have access to this application. Children under the age of 18 will not be given access nor account credentials to utilize. This is not allowed by our institution at this time.

Data Collection Policy

For detailed technical information about data collection methods, see our Data Collection Policy.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or your data, please contact us at MINDGamePack@mgb.org.
