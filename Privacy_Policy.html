Privacy Policy for MIND GamePack

Last updated: October 21, 2025

The MIND GamePack application is used exclusively for research purposes and is distributed privately to study participants on provisioned devices managed by our facility. By using this application, you consent to the collection and use of your de-identified game data as described below.

What Data We Collect

  • De-identified gameplay data and survey responses related to cognitive performance.
  • No personally identifiable information (PII) is collected or stored by the application.

How Data Is Stored

  • Data is transmitted securely to servers managed by our research facility, including a local secure database and a cloud instance.
  • Data may be temporarily cached on the device if a Wi-Fi connection is unavailable, and will be transmitted once a connection is restored.

How Data Is Used and Shared

  • De-identified data is used for research purposes to assess cognitive functioning.
  • By participating, you consent to your de-identified data being shared with third parties and other researchers for scientific purposes.
  • No data is used for commercial purposes.

Withdrawal and Data Deletion

  • You may stop using the application and withdraw your consent at any time by notifying the study team.
  • Upon withdrawal, no further data will be collected. You may request deletion of your data, though all data is de-identified and cannot be linked to you personally.

Children's Privacy

  • This application is intended for adult research participants only and is not approved for use by children.
