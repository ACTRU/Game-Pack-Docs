Frequently Asked Questions

Who can use this app?

This app is designed for participants enrolled in MGH research studies. Access requires study credentials provided by the research team.

I'm having technical issues

Please contact the study team at MINDGamePack@mgb.org with details about the issue.

How do I get my study credentials?

Study credentials are provided by the research coordinator when you enroll in the study.
